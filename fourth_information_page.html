The Simple Machines

the simple machines tthat are used in the running shoes

There are a few simples machines used in running shoes.
These simple machines include:

The Wedge

The raised heel acts as a wedge to increase comforft and heel support.

The Lever

The midsole of the running shoe acts as a lever by compressing and releasing to add pressure and energy to the foot helping to propel the foot forward.
